Color Scheme

We chose a color palette that is different shades of blue. It ties into the weather theme and provides a fun atmosphere to the veiwer.

A nice dark blue for the h1 headers

And a complementary lighter blue for the h2 headers

Then a simple black that is legible and easy to read for the main paragraphs.

We then used a the nice darker blue for the header, a white for the main and footer, and the light blue for the links.
