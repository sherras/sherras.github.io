Typography

For the headings and titles we chose the font Alga. This is a nice readable font, but it is also fun and provides a happy tone to the site. This font also corresponds to the logo which ties it in nicely.

For the paragraph font we chose Rubik. This font is a simple, but cute, sans serif font. It is easily legible and keeps your eyes flowing.
