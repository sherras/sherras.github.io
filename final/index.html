We are so excited to have you here with us! Please enjoy this webiste! We promise to keep you up to date on the current weather conditions, as well as help you know what weather conditions may be for these areas of Idaho for the next five days. The towns that we are proud to call home are Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. These beautiful towns of Idaho are wonderful places to live and visit. If you never have been to them before please check them out and take a look at the events that are coming up for them. Preston is our beautiful little farm town, if you are looking for a great hometown rural feel, a place to get away and relax preston is the town to go to. Do you love the adventures of hiking up a cold mountain just to get to a warm hot spring and enjoy the different aspects of nature? Soda Springs would be the place to be. Are you a fish lover and want to get catch your self some big ones? Check out Fish Haven! Be sure to look at the weather and plan accordingly! We would love to see you here. Welcome to our Idaho family!
