Top Tips to Get Big Fish

Fish Haven really does live up to it's name, but how exactly do you catch not only a lot of fish, but BIG fish? We have a lot of fishing experts around here and we took the time to ask them what they do to catch those whopper fish. Here is what we found.

Use cheese as your bait. Little do most people know, but fish LOVE thier cheese. If you need to get some when you come, hit up the cheese factory in Preston on your way! If for some reason the fish still aren't biting, they probably already had lunch and are looking for something a little sweeter, tyr throwing a marshmallow on that hook and see what you get! ;)
Get rid of the silence. Did you know that fish actually like noise and that the silence trick is an old fisherman's myth? The best way to catch the big fish is to get a newspaper, roll it up, and yell through it, "Here fishy fishy!" And there you go, they will be your way in no time!
Last, but not least. Let your pole rest on it's own. Find a place in your boat and just prop it up. Not only does this help you not be impatient and reel it back in to recast, but it also give a stillness to the line that the big fish like, but be careful to keep an eye on it or your pole may be gone when you turn around.

We hope these tips are useful to you and help you be successful in all your upcoming fishing adventures! If you have any more tips, please feel free to send them to our email: theweatherheather@twh.com.
