Best Places to Stay in Preston

Looking for a calm relaxing getaway? Preston is full of wonderful places to stay. We have anything from outdoor tent camping to luxury air bnb's. The number one rated tent camping place here is at the Impreston Camp ground on Mountain Hill road. They provide restrooms and wonderful places to put up a tent, start a fire, grill some burgers, and enjoy nature. They also have wonderful hiking trails you can explore. The bet RV park is called Parking in Preston on Viewside lane. With all the hookups you need to stay comfy in your nice trailer and a great staff to support you, you will not regret staying there. Looking for a nice Hotel? The Cattle Ranch Inn is the top rated in town. With an old timey vibe you will feel like you are walking into the old wild west (but with electricity and plumbing;). Wanting to rent a nice place with more privacy? Check out the Preston Life Air BnB. Right in the mountain, but close to town, you will feel right at home in nature here.

These are not all the wonderful places to stay, just a few. You can look at the Preston Town website to find a list of more places you could come to enjoy your vacation away from the city! Be sure to check out our upcoming events list and see if these places have any discounts for the special event. We cannot await your arrival!
